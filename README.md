# 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard | Google Sheets

## 📌 Project Overview
The Bank Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Google Sheets project designed to analyze customer retention and churn behavior within a banking environment. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer demographics, account information, product usage, and activity status to identify factors contributing to customer attrition.

By transforming raw customer data into meaningful visual insights, this dashboard helps business stakeholders understand churn patterns and develop strategies to improve customer retention and engagement.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall customer churn performance.
- Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
- Compare churn trends across different countries.
- Understand the impact of age, product ownership, and activity status on churn.
- Support data-driven decision-making for customer retention strategies.

---

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### Customer Churn Overview
- Total Customers: **10,000**
- Churned Customers: **2,037**
- Overall Churn Rate: **20.4%**
- Retained Customers: **79.6%**

### Churn by Product Usage
- Customers with **3 and 4 products** show significantly higher churn rates.
- Customers with **1 or 2 products** have stronger retention levels.

### Churn by Country
- **France** has the highest number of churned customers.
- **Germany** and **Spain** have comparatively lower churn volumes.

### Churn by Age Group
- Customers aged **41–50 years** represent the highest churn segment.
- The **31–40 age group** contains the largest customer population with relatively better retention.

### Churn by Activity Status
- Inactive customers are more likely to leave the bank.
- Active customers demonstrate higher retention and loyalty.
